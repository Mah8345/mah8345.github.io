my interests

I’m deeply fascinated by the synergy of hardware and intelligent software, where embedded systems serve as the backbone for smart, real-world applications. My passion lies in designing responsive firmware for microcontrollers, crafting autonomous robotics, and implementing advanced control strategies like PID and state-space control to bring dynamic systems to life. I’m equally drawn to machine learning, where I explore its potential for sensor fusion, computer vision, and adaptive control—pushing the boundaries of what embedded and robotic systems can achieve. At the core of it all, I thrive on bridging algorithms with physical hardware to create efficient, intelligent solutions.
